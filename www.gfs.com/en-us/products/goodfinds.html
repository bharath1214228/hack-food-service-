Scout. Seek. Savor.

Goodfinds by Gordon is a program that searches high and low to find meaningful food solutions that not only taste good, but do good too. From ethically sourced ingredients, to the latest food technology advancements, to sustainable packaging—we’ve gathered the best of the best products and brands to share with you. Because we believe the way we choose our food really can change the world.

An Introduction to our Goodfinds by Gordon Program

Purpose-Driven

We believe in companies and products that stand for, and do, more. Because we believe it’s up to us to make the world a better place. So, we made it our mission to gather purposeful, innovative food solutions that drive a massive change for good.

People-Focused

From environmentalists to health-conscious eaters to urban farmers—our goal is to provide you with the niche food solutions that put your needs, and the needs of future generations, first. Here, we advocate for those who demand more when it comes to their food. We are constantly searching for companies that deliver the best option, not the easiest one.

Planet-Forward

We only have one planet, so we keep sustainability at the forefront of every decision we make. Our goal is to positively affect the environment with the companies we partner with. Because, when our planet is taken care of, our food is too.
